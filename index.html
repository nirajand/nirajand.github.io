Achievements

Winner in Science Exhibition

I led my team to victory in the inaugural Science Exhibition Competition held in Devdaha in 2017, proudly representing Green Plant Boarding School. We emerged triumphant in the Science exhibition organized in 3rd National Industrial Trade and Commerce Festival on Feb 7, 2017 - Feb 23, 2017.

Our award-winning project, Smart City - Devdaha, showcased our innovative approach to urban planning, harnessing resources efficiently to achieve a carbon-neutral city, with a focus on optimized utilization of electricity, infrastructure, and water.

Runner Up in OnCampus Hult Prize Gandaki University Idea Pitching Competition - 2024

Our team, WRAP, stood runner up in the Idea Pitching Competition organized on February 23, 2024. Our innovative idea, was to refurbish the waste plastic wrappers and create handbags, wallets, mosaic art from plastic and other accessories to reduce plastic waste and promote sustainable living. Our team was recognized for our creativity, vision, and commitment to social impact.

Extra Experiences

Founding President of Children's Club of Pragati, Devdaha

In August 2016, I sparked an initiative to establish a Children's Club in my hometown, Khaireni. I brought together a talented group of young minds, and through collaborative effort, we turned the concept into a thriving reality. As the founding president, I provided strategic direction, driving the club's growth and development until April 2017.

Treasurer at Green Plant Red Cross Society

In 2073 B.S. (May 2015), I was at the forefront of a pioneering effort at Green Plant Boarding School, my alma mater, as we launched the school's inaugural Red Cross Society. My leadership skills and dedication earned me the esteemed role of Treasurer, a position I proudly held.

Founding Secretary of Mathematics and IT Club at Global Collegiate School

In May 2019, I had the privilege of being part of a milestone moment at Global Collegiate School, as the Mathematics and IT Club was formally established. I served as the Founding Secretary of the club until November 2021, playing a key role in shaping the club's vision and direction. During my tenure, I was instrumental in organizing various events and activities that fostered a love for mathematics and IT among students. One of the highlights was our celebration of International Pi Day on March 14, 2021, where we organized a thrilling competition to test students' knowledge of the iconic mathematical constant, π. The competition was a huge success, with students vying to recall the maximum number of digits of pi, and the winners were awarded for their exceptional math skills.

Representing Gandaki University as Student Ambassador for Annual Nepal AI School 2023 at NAAMII

In April 2023, NAAMII (NepAl Applied Mathematics and Informatics Institute for Research) sought student ambassadors to represent their colleges and contribute to the success of the Nepal AI School. I'm thrilled that my application was selected from a competitive pool, and I now represent Gandaki University as a Student Ambassador. This role recognizes my passion for AI and innovation, and I'm committed to working with NAAMII to make the event a memorable experience for all. I'm honored to be part of this initiative and look forward to contributing to the growth of Nepal's AI community.

Lead Graphics Designer at Hult Prize Gandaki University 2024

I was honored to be appointed as the Lead Graphics Designer for the executive year of 2023-24, when Hult Prize was introduced to Gandaki University for the first time. Alongside my talented team members, Rakshya Giri and Kripa Khadka, we were responsible for designing the graphical aspects of the OnCampus Idea Pitching Competition event, bringing the vision to life.

Secretary at Dynamic Public Library (DPL) Pokhara

I took on the role of Secretary at DPL in January 2024, an organization committed to fostering a culture of reading and positive thinking, guided by the motto "पठन संस्कृतिको विकास सँगै सकारात्मक सोच सहितको सु-संस्कृत युवा नेतृत्व " (Nurturing visionary leaders with a positive mindset, rooted in a culture of reading and lifelong learning). As a Secretary, I have been actively involved in organizing various programs and events. Notably, I participated in our Speaker's Corner Program, and contributed to the success of our two-week long book collection campaign. Additionally, I played a key role in organizing the "Scientific Writing and Communication Workshop", led by esteemed guest speaker Dr. Rajkumar Thapa, Executive Director of the Gandaki University Research Center, Invention and Innovation Center. This experience has been instrumental in honing my organizational and leadership skills.
